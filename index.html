Courses We Offer

B.Tech

In the 4 years of the under graduation program, you will learn about the different types of manufacturing plants of the textile goods and apparel industry, machinery, and management of the plants. Although the different college has a different curriculum, the first year of the college you will have introduction courses of all core branches.

M.Tech

M Tech (Masters of Technology) is a 2-year postgraduate professional degree in engineering. The aspirants are required to have a bachelor’s degree in technology for joining the M Tech course with a minimum of 50% marks from a recognized university. The course can be accomplished based on a full-time or part-time course depending upon the preference of the student.

PHD

However, there is an option to pursue an online PhD from this University. The candidates do not need to go out of the country and they can complete their PhD degree by staying in their home country. The Universities offer online PhD in various fields like engineering, nursing, education, design, medicine, science, arts, etc.
