The World Wide Web

The WORLD WIDE WEB or Simply the web, has been a 'Killer App' of the Internet.Certainly it's capability to display text and graphics and provide access to other pages and information has made the fastest growing components of the internet.Major online service are American Online, CompuService and Prodigy.

Get Connected

A way to access is to get an account with an internet service provider , or ISP. These include access to the world wide web and other internet resources, and often provide space to store web pages one will create . A very helpful feature of the web is the capability to move from page by page by selecting specific highlighted words and phrases or images , which are called as links.
