    Tata Harrier

    Tata Harrier is a 5 seater SUV available in a price range of ₹ 14.69 - 22.20 Lakh. It is available in 28 variants, 1956 cc engine option and 2 transmission options : Manual and Automatic (TC). Other key specifications of the Harrier include a Ground Clearance of 205 mm, Kerb Weight of 1655 kg and Bootspace of 425 litres. The Harrier is available in 8 colours. The mileage of Harrier ranges from 14.6 kmpl to 16.3 kmpl.
